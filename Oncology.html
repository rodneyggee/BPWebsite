Oncology

Only full time employees of BP Southern Africa (Pty) Ltd are eligible to join BP Medical Aid Society as a principal member.

Our Oncology Programme is specifically designed to actively empower patients. Participants on this Programme generally have a more positive attitude and approach to treating their condition and improving general wellness. This positive attitude often translates into improved treatment compliance and a healthier lifestyle, which can make a real difference in the way you respond to treatment and your general quality of life.

call(0800) 001 607 request to be transferred to the Oncology Department

Care Coach

The Programme offers you direct access to a specialised oncology care coach. Your care coach will work closely with you, your doctor and oncologist to ensure care is co-ordinated, and you receive maximum value from your medical scheme.

Care Coachclose

The Programme offers you direct access to a specialised oncology care coach. Your care coach will work closely with you, your doctor and oncologist to ensure care is co-ordinated, and you receive maximum value from your medical scheme.

  • Your care coach will also
  • Pre-authorise your oncology treatment and, where appropriate, refer to an oncology medical advisor for evaluation.
  • Where necessary, draw on the support of a specialised clinical team, which includes independent advisors and experts from the South African Oncology Consortium (SAOC) and ISIMO Health.
  • Work with all healthcare providers involved in your treatment, ensuring your care is coordinated, hassle-free and your health outcomes are as positive as possible provide counselling and refer you to support groups.
  • Encourage you to follow a healthy lifestyle, which will not only help you cope better but also pump up your energy levels.
  • Assess your medication against regularly updated treatment guidelines and protocols, to ensure you have affordable access to the most appropriate medication
Icon Healthcare Provider Network

BP Medical Aid Society has appointed the Independant Clinical Oncology Network (ICON) as our DSP for the Provision of Oncology Services.

Scheme Benefits

Most types of Cancer are covered by your Medical Benefits under Prescribed Minimum Benefits(PMB), where you will be covered completely for the diagnosis and treatment as longs as it complies with legislation. Your Care Coach can advise on these requirements.

Not all types of cancer meet the requirements of being a PMB, in this situation your Care Coach can advise how to use your Medical Benefits in the best way to treat your condition.
